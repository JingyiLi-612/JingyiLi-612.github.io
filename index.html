About Me

I'm Jingyi LI, a PhD student in the GeomeriX team supervised by Steve OUDOT at École Polytechnique and Inria Saclay. Before that, I obtained my undergraduate degree in computer science from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.

My research focuses on the theoretical foundation and computation of topological data analysis (TDA), with a particular emphasis on multi-parameter persistent homology.
